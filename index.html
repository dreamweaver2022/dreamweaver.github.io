What is ENS?

Ethereum Name Service domains are secure domain names for the decentralized world.
ENS domains provide a way for users to map human readable names to blockchain
and non-blockchain resources, like Ethereum addresses, IPFS hashes, or website URLs.
ENS domains can be purchased or sold on secondary NFT Marketplaces.

How can you get one?

Connect your wallet and register your own ENS domain name now at the offical ENS website: app.ens.domains

For more information about ENS Domains, visit the official ENS documentation: docs.ens.domains

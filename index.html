What is ENS?

Ethereum Name Service domains are secure domain names for the decentralized world.
ENS domains provide a way for users to map human readable names to blockchain
and non-blockchain resources, like Ethereum addresses, IPFS hashes, or website URLs.
ENS domains can be bought and sold on secondary markets as NFT's.
(For more information about ENS Domains, visit the official ENS website: ENS Domains documentation).
